## Professional Auto Glass Repair

When your auto glass is damaged, you need quick, professional repair. Our experienced technicians handle all types of glass damage, from small cracks to complex repairs.

### Types of Repairs We Handle

- **Stone Chips:** Remove the damage and seal it before it spreads
- **Small Cracks:** Repair cracks up to 12 inches using professional resin injection
- **Edge Damage:** Fix damage near the edges where it's most critical
- **Laminated Glass:** Repair the outer layers of safety glass

### Why Repair Instead of Replace?

If the damage is caught early, repair is often the best solution:

- **Cost-effective:** Repairs are significantly cheaper than replacement
- **Faster service:** Most repairs take 1-2 hours
- **Preserves your glass:** Your original OEM glass stays intact
- **Insurance friendly:** Many repairs are covered by your insurance with little to no deductible

### Our Repair Process

1. **Damage Assessment:** We evaluate the location, size, and type of damage
2. **Preparation:** Clean the damaged area thoroughly
3. **Resin Injection:** Apply specialized resin into the damaged area
4. **Curing:** Allow the resin to set and cure properly
5. **Finishing:** Polish the repair to restore clarity

### When Replacement is Needed

Some damage can't be repaired. We'll be honest about when your glass needs to be replaced rather than repaired. Safety always comes first.
