## Windshield Replacement Services

When your windshield is too damaged to repair, we provide professional replacement using quality glass that meets or exceeds OEM specifications.

### Why Choose Professional Replacement?

Your windshield is more than just a window—it's a critical safety component that:

- Supports the vehicle's structural integrity
- Helps deploy airbags properly
- Provides weather protection
- Maintains cabin pressure

Improper installation can compromise all of these functions. That's why professional installation matters.

### OEM vs. Aftermarket Options

**OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer)**
- Exact match to your vehicle's original glass
- Highest quality standard
- Best for luxury or high-value vehicles
- Higher cost

**Aftermarket**
- Quality replacement glass
- Meets safety standards
- More affordable
- Perfect for most vehicles

### Our Replacement Process

1. **Removal:** Carefully remove the old windshield without damaging the frame
2. **Preparation:** Clean and prep the frame for new glass
3. **Installation:** Install new glass using automotive-grade adhesive
4. **Sealing:** Apply proper sealing and weatherstripping
5. **Curing:** Allow adhesive to cure before the vehicle is driven

### Insurance Coverage

Most insurance policies cover windshield replacement with little or no deductible. We work directly with insurance companies to simplify the process.
