## Windshield Crack Repair

A cracked windshield is dangerous and illegal in California. We specialize in professional crack repair to restore your windshield's integrity.

### Types of Cracks We Repair

- **Star breaks:** Small cracks radiating from impact point
- **Linear cracks:** Straight cracks across windshield
- **Bullseye chips:** Circular damage with a cone shape
- **Half-moon chips:** Crescent-shaped damage
- **Edge cracks:** Damage near windshield edges

### Why Repair Now?

- **Safety:** Windshield supports airbag deployment
- **Cost:** Repair is much cheaper than replacement
- **Speed:** Most repairs take 1-2 hours
- **Insurance:** Often fully covered with low deductible

### Repair Requirements

**We can repair:**
- Damage outside driver's vision area
- Cracks less than 12 inches long
- Chips less than 1 inch

**Replacement needed if:**
- Damage in driver's direct line of sight
- Multiple cracks
- Damage on windshield edges
- Cracks longer than 12 inches

### Professional Repair Process

Using advanced resin injection technology:

1. Clean damaged area
2. Inject high-strength resin
3. Allow proper curing
4. Polish and finish
