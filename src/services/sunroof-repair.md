## Sunroof Repair & Replacement

Sunroofs add luxury and functionality to your vehicle. When they're damaged, professional repair is essential to restore both function and value.

### Types of Sunroof Damage

- **Cracked glass:** Compromises weatherproofing
- **Broken motor:** Panel won't open/close
- **Seal failure:** Water leaks into cabin
- **Frame damage:** Structural issues
- **Broken tracks:** Panel won't slide properly

### Repair Options

**Glass Repair/Replacement**
- Replace just the glass panel
- Maintain original look
- Restore functionality

**Motor Repair**
- Fix motor mechanisms
- Restore power operation
- Often more affordable than replacement

**Seal Replacement**
- Stop water leaks
- Restore weatherproofing
- Prevent interior damage

**Full Sunroof Replacement**
- When damage is extensive
- New mechanism and glass
- Full warranty

### Why Professional Service?

Sunroofs are complex systems requiring:
- Specialized tools
- Technical expertise
- Proper sealing knowledge
- Quality parts

DIY repairs often result in water leaks and further damage.

### Process

1. Comprehensive damage assessment
2. Determine repair vs. replacement
3. Remove damaged components
4. Install new parts or repair mechanisms
5. Test seals and operation
6. Verify weatherproofing
