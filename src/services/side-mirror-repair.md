## Side Mirror Repair & Replacement

Damaged side mirrors affect your visibility and safety. We repair or replace side mirrors quickly and affordably.

### Common Mirror Issues

- **Cracked mirror glass:** Reduce visibility and safety
- **Broken mirror housing:** Damaged frame or bracket
- **Mirror motor failure:** Power mirrors not working
- **Mirror peeling:** Reflective coating deteriorating

### Repair vs. Replacement

**We repair:**
- Cracked mirror glass (replace only the reflective surface)
- Minor housing damage
- Motor problems (when salvageable)

**We replace:**
- Severely damaged housings
- Power mirrors beyond repair
- Missing pieces

### Mirror Types

**Manual Mirrors**
- Simple, durable
- No moving parts
- Lower cost

**Power Mirrors**
- Convenient adjustment
- Motor may fail
- Higher replacement cost

**Heated Mirrors**
- Defrost capability
- More complex
- Professional service recommended

### Process

1. Assess damage
2. Quote repair or replacement
3. Remove damaged mirror
4. Install new or repaired mirror
5. Test function and alignment
